Carbon Clean

General Information

About The Problem

The problem we have identified is the general lack of awareness about what climate change is and how it affects people’s lives.

About The App

Knowing that our community has a strong fondness for social media and games, our idea is to create an app that teaches them about climate change and its affects throught various games and quizzes to help them build important habits that will be vital to securing a carbon clean future for our planet.

About The Target User

This of course would be mainly targeted to pupils in secondary schools as we are the future inhabitants of this planet and if we don't learn now how to live a sustainable life then 10 - 20 years later we will be back to square one fighting the climate battle regardless of what ingenious solution the world leaders will come up with in November.

About How It Works

The quizzes and habit goals earn you in-game currency which is used to buy avatars and social icons, this is so they can blast their social media accounts with their progress and ranks. In doing so it will make living a sustainable life a shared goal which teens compete for bragging rights and social status.

About The Team

We are a team of Rosshall Academy's finest tech enthusiasts. As well as entering competitions and showcasing our digital skills such as coding, designing, team leadership, we also help our school community become more acustomed to the digital technology currently available in our school.

Profile Features

Avatar & Badges

Users are able to see their inventory of purchased avatars and badges. From this they are able to select and customize their profile and share it on social media.

Shop

The shop button is located top-left of the screen. When users click on this link it will take them to the shop screen.

Edit Details

The two bars on the top-right of the screen, when clicked, will open up a modal where they can alter their account details such as changing a password or location

Carbon Clean

Stats Page

Carbon Footprint Data

50% of the screen will show you your carbon footprint data eg. current yearly footprint, improvements highlighted on a graph, etc

Goals

50% of the screen will show you your goals data, it will show you your current goal and your progress on it and it will show you your completed goals. By showing you your completed goals it will give you a sense of achievement that will fuel your motivation to continue useing the app.

Edit / Create

The two bars, when clicked, will open up a modal where you can re-do your carbon footprint test or create new goals for the week.

Carbon Clean

Quizzes & Games

Daily Quizzes

This sub-section of the screen will display quizzes that have just been released. The daily quizzes will reward you with double the amount of coins you would get from an older quiz. This will help generate consistency of how often users use the app.

Top Earning

Quizzes vary in difficulty, the top earning ones will be difficult but will earn you more coins than a standard easy quiz. This amount will never surpass the reward value of a Daily Quiz though.

User's Balance

The top right of the screen will show the user's current coin balance

Game Page

Background Image

This will show you a relevant image to the quiz being played

Multiple Choice Quiz

Currently the only game type is a multiple choice quiz, this game simplicity is so that we have time to work on other areas of the app. Once the core features are implemented, we will come back to the game screen and create different game types to make the game feature more interesting.

Shop Page

Top Picks

The top picks section will show a selection of items that the developer team like.

Pricing

All the tiles showcasing avatars and badges have a price value on their bottom-right. When the tile is selected it will ask the user for confirmation before purchasing the item. This is so they don't accidently spend their money on an item they do not like.

Coin Balance

The top-right of the screen shows the user's current coin balance so they know how much they can spend in the digital shop.
