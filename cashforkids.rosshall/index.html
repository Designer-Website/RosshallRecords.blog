Cash for kids: Mission Christmas

Cash for Kids is a charity which gives grants to vulnerable children across the UK who need it. Cash for kids work with other organisations to provide support, hot meals and warm clothes to these families. They believe that ‘all children should be able to express their individuality, achieve their potential and live life to the full.’ 

In 2019, Cash for Kids raised around £20 million and supported over 1 million families and children. 2020 has changed everything. As families unexpectedly face poverty, more and more children could be waking up on Christmas Day without anything under the tree. You can be a Santa
